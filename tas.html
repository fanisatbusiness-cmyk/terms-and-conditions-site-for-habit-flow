Privacy Policy

  • Habit Tracking Data: Your habits and progress are stored to provide core app functionality.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide core features like habit tracking and reminders.
  • To let you sign in and keep your account safe.
  • To display ads and keep the app free.
  • To show your posts in the community tab.

What We Don't Do

  • We don’t sell your personal information to third parties.
  • We don’t track you outside of the app.

Your Choices

  • You can request account deletion by emailing us.
  • You can reset or opt out of ad personalization in your device settings.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your data, but no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.
